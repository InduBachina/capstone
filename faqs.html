FAQs

What is a balanced diet?

A balanced diet includes a variety of foods in the right proportions, consuming adequate amounts of nutrients necessary for good health.

How much water should I drink daily?

It's commonly recommended to drink eight 8-ounce glasses of water a day, which equals about 2 liters or half a gallon. However, individual needs can vary.

What are the benefits of regular exercise?

Regular exercise helps improve overall health, reduces the risk of chronic diseases, aids in weight management, and boosts mental health.
